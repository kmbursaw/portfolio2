Fly the Unfriendly Skies

The goal of this project was to see in what Air Route Traffic Control Center the most unauthorized drone flights occured. The data was taken from the FAA website and collated from 2018-2022. The finished product was intended for use by both pilots and controllers alike to aid with deconfliction and safety.
Made for Geog 370 Introduction to Cartography.
