Who I am

Hello

My name is Kevin Bursaw and I have a passion for Aviation, Geography, and Big Data! In particular Cartography and GIS. I love being able to take complex problems and datasets and vizualize them for better understanding. This started with my training as a tactician, making tactical maps for aircerws, and has carried on since. My goal is to take large aviation datasets and visualize them for better planning and sustainability of the industry, and using these products to trian the next generation of pilots to help fill the pilot shortage.
